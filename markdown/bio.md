## Timeline

I was born in Wisconsin on May 25 2007. My parents moved to New York, where my brother was born, but eventually moved back to their home state of California after the 2008 financial crash. They stayed in my grandmother's house for a while, then moved to Ventura, a small beach town. 

This is where my childhood was. We lived on the bottom floor of a duplex an a street directly connected to the beach. Naturally, much of my time was spent on that beach. Boogie boarding, playing in the sand, riding bikes. We would never own a house there, however. Coastal real estate is extraordinarily expensive. This, combined with more work opportunities for my father led to us moving to Fresno in 2016. I was 9.

In the center of the central valley, we could afford a house of our own in a good neighborhood, complete with a yard and pool. I attended a private Catholic school into junior high, at which point my mother decided to homeschool me. Why? For one thing, my mother already stayed home, so she was able to educate her two children personally. Additionally, my brother and I (especially me) didn't fit in the education system. Neither of us are geniuses by any means, but we were high enough above the average that school became completely uninteresting. 

I struggle to choose my path in high school. I simply didn't know what I wanted to do. I ended up applying to several colleges, and choosing to attend Cal State University at Channel Islands (CSUCI). The location was under 30 minutes away from my home town, and around 10 minutes from the beach. 

That's where I am now. I'm still not sure about my future, but I have confidence in my self.
